Qiayuan Liao

I'm a second-year Ph.D. student at Hybrid Robotics, UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Koushil Sreenath.

Currently, I'm a research intern at Boston Dynamics under Scott Kuindersma on reinforcement-learning control for the E-Atlas.

I'm a full-stack roboticist dedicated to creating systems that achieve agile and robust locomotion and manipulation. Toward this goal, I focus on: (a) Developing novel machines and actuations; (b) Combining learning and model-based methods; (c) Co-designing hardware, learning and control.
